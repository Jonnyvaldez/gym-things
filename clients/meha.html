Hack Squat
3 sets10 reps
Cue: Start at machine weight (~80lb) — check form before adding. Feet hip-width on platform. Control the descent, pause at bottom before driving up.
Reverse Lunge
3 sets10 reps / leg
Cue: Step back until both knees reach 90°. Front foot stays flat. Drive through your front heel to stand. Add dumbbells when bodyweight feels easy.
Machine Shoulder Press
3 sets10–12 reps
Cue: Adjust seat so handles start at shoulder height. Press overhead — keep a slight bend at the top, don't fully lock out.
Seated Cable Row
3 sets10–12 reps
Cue: Sit tall throughout — don't hunch. Pull handle to lower ribs, squeeze shoulder blades together at the finish.
Side Plank
2 sets30 sec / side
Cue: Hip lifted, straight line from head to feet. Stack or stagger feet. Complete each side fully before switching.
Bird Dog
2 sets10 reps
Cue: On all fours, hands under shoulders. Extend opposite arm and leg — back flat, hips square. Slow and controlled wins here.
