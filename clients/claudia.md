## Week 1 Program

### Day 1 — Lower Body A (Glute + Quad + Ham)

| # | Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest | Notes |
|---|----------|------|------|------|-------|
| 1 | Hip Thrust (barbell or Smith) | 3 | 6–8 | 3 min | Drive through heels, full hip extension, squeeze 2 sec at top |
| 2 | Hack Squat or Leg Press | 3 | 8–10 | 2.5 min | Feet low on platform, shoulder width, full depth |
| 3 | RDL (DB or barbell) | 3 | 8–10 | 2.5 min | Push hips back, slight knee bend, stretch hamstrings, controlled lowering |
| 4 | Leg Extension | 2 | 12–15 | 2 min | Slight pause at top, squeeze quads, controlled lowering |
| 5 | Cable Kickback | 2 | 12–15/side | 1.5 min | Slight forward lean, squeeze glute at top, no low back arch |

**Total: 13 sets | ~45 min**

| # | Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest | Notes |

## Progression Notes

- **Weeks 1–4:** Find working weights, nail form, build consistency. If hitting top of rep range on all sets, increase weight 5 lbs next session.
- **Weeks 5–8:** Push intensity on compounds. Add 1 set to any compound if recovery is good. Day 5 — add ankle weights or bands to barre movements.
- **Weeks 9–12:** Peak phase. Heaviest loads on compounds, maintain accessories. Deload week 12 if needed before summer.
